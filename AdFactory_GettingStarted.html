Path A — Produce Your Own Ad
Make an ad for your own offer using Pinky Floyd or ARIA
1
Install the updated skill
In Claude Desktop → click "Copy to your skills" on the ad-production-bible.skill file → restart Claude Desktop. This gives you the intake button menus.
2
Add your photos to the Photo Vault
Drop your Pinky Floyd photos into your HTML/PHOTO VAULT/Pinky Floyd/ folder. ARIA photos → ARIA folder. These are what you upload to Whisk for each scene.
3
Open Claude Desktop and say "make me an ad"
The skill pops up button menus — tap your answers for format, platform, tone, voice, and style. Claude builds your full production document automatically.
4
Open your Ad Factory HTML file
Claude saves it to your HTML folder. Open it in Brave and bookmark it. Your scenes, Whisk prompts, and progress tracker are all right there.
5
Generate images in Whisk — Scene by Scene
Click 🖼️ Open Whisk on each scene → upload your photo from the Photo Vault → paste the Whisk prompt (copy button is right there) → generate. Save the image.
6
Run each image through Google Flow
Click ▶️ Open Flow → upload your Whisk image → paste the Flow direction → render your 8-second clip. ⚠️ Spoken voice only — NO text overlays in Flow.
7
Assemble in CapCut
Click ✂️ Open CapCut → import all your Flow clips in scene order → add music, captions, and your CTA text overlay HERE (not in Flow) → export and post. 👑
🖼️ Whisk ▶️ Flow ✂️ CapCut 🎬 Ad Factory

Path B — Deliver a DFY Client Order ($1,500)
Intake → Build → Deliver the production bible to your client
1
Send client the intake questions
Ask them: offer name, platform, tone (Funny & Sassy / Sassy Professional / GlamTwin), visual style (Beyoncé / Designer Suit / etc.), their photo(s), solo or two-person format.
2
Open Claude and paste their answers
Say "make me an ad" and provide their brief. The skill builds the full 6-section production document for every scene automatically.
3
Review the script before building HTML
Read through all scenes. Check word counts (max 10 per dialogue scene). Make sure there's a B-Roll scene. Approve or tweak before Claude generates the HTML file.
4
Have Claude build the Ad Factory HTML
Say "now build the HTML file." Claude creates an AdFactory_[ClientOffer].html file in your HTML folder with everything — scenes, Whisk prompts, Flow directions, checkboxes, progress tracker.
5
Deliver the HTML file to your client
Send them the HTML file + a voice note walking them through it. That IS the $1,500 deliverable — their complete production bible, ready to execute.
6
Time your delivery — log it!
Track how long steps 2–5 take. This is your turnaround time for marketing the offer. Most DFY production bibles take 45–90 min once your process is dialed in.
